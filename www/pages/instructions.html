Instructions
Step 1: Go to the Settings page and add the contact details of the people you want to send an email to when you get into the taxi.
Step 2: After saving your contacts, press Continue and you will be brought to the Verify Page.
Step 3: On the homepage you will be required to enter the taxi drivers registration number and accept him or decline him.
Step 4: After accepting the taxi you will be brought to a page of contacts that you added in Step 1
Step 5: Select the contact you want to alert that you are leaving your current destination and on the way to your next destination.
Step 6: Select the contact you want to alert as you are arriving, or have arrived at your destination.
